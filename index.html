Description:

  • The length of the lines drawn from earth describe the distance of the exoplanet from the earth in light years
  • The size of the circle showcases the size of the exo planet with respect to the size of the earth
  • The opacity or luminous of the planet/circle describes the brightness of the exoplanet seen from earth
  • The Play and Stop button are respectively used to pause and resume the animation.
  • The Data points box is used to enter the number of data points are desired to be shown in the above visualization.
    • 3 Attributes:
    1. Distance of the Exo planet from earth is shown by the length of the lines
    2. stellar_magnitude: Brightness of the planet, the brighter the planet the show cased by opacity
    3. Radius w.r.t to earth is shown by the size of the circle
